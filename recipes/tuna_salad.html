Tuna Salad

Description

Delicious, quick and fresh salad for summer

Ingredientes

  • Can white tuna packed in water, drained
  • Can whole kernel corn, drained
  • Pickled gherkins
  • Untoasted walnuts halves
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice pickles and walnuts
  2. Gather them with tuna and corn kernels in a salad bowl. Stir everything
  3. Add vinegar and olive oil
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Put it on the fridge for al least an hour
