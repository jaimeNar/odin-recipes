Oven-Baked Salmon

Description

Simple baked salmon with potatos and green peppers

Ingredientes

  • A half salmon tail
  • Potatos
  • Green peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 210 dregrees C
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into this slices
  3. Remove the seeds from the peppers and cut them into this slices as well
  4. Add salt and pepper to the salmon
